Fuction Overloading


Create two overloaded inline functions area( ) 
to compute (i) area of triangle and (ii) area of circle.

Create 4 overloaded functions min( ) to
compute minimum of 3 parameters passed :

(i)	int min(int , int, int );
(ii)	float min(float, float, float);
(iii)	char min(char, char, char);
(iv)	double min(double, double, double);

Create 2 overloaded functions calc_interest( ) 
to compute simple interest and compound interest.
